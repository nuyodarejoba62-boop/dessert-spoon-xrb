​Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

When you're on our site, we might get a hold of two kinds of info:

*   **Personal Stuff:**

    *   This is stuff you give us directly, like your name, email, address, etc.
    *   Basically if you fill out a form, drop a comment, or sign up for something, that's what we're talking about.
*   **Techy Stuff:**

    *   This is the sort of thing that gets picked up automatically like your IP address, what kind of browser you're using, your operating system, and the type of device.
    *   Also which site sent you here, what pages you look at, and generally where you're located (without getting too specific).
    *   This is great to help us understand how people like you use the website and make things even better.

**2. How We Use It All**

The info we get is for things like:

*   Keeping the website running smoothly, and making it better all the time.
*   Answering your questions and responding to messages.
*   Sending you newsletters or cool updates (but only if you asked for them).
*   Figuring out what's working, and what's not, on the site.
*   Making sure the website's safe and sound.
*   Following the rules and stopping bad stuff from happening.

we grab what we need &amp; use, but we never share or sell it without asking you first.

**3. Cookies and All That**

Yup, we use cookies and other similar stuff to make the site work better. Cookies are basically little files that hang out on your computer and help us remember what you like, track traffic, and just make your experience more personal.

You can turn cookies off in your browser, but heads up, it might make some of the site features not work so well.

*   **Essential cookies**: Help the website operation.
*   **Performance cookies**: Help us understand user operation.
*   **Functional cookies**: Help us remember user choices.
*   **Analytics cookies**: Track people (like Google analytics

**4. Keeping Your Data Safe**

We're serious about keeping your info private. We've got security measures to try and stop anyone from snooping around, making changes, or wrecking things.

Even though we're doing our best, just remember that nothing online is ever 100% secure. We can't promise perfection, but we're seriously trying.

**5. Sharing (or Not Sharing)**

We’re not in the business of selling, trading, or handing over your personal info.

The only times we might share stuff are:

*   If we're using a service to keep the website running (like hosting, analytics, email). They have to promise to keep things secret.
*   If the law tells us we have to.
*   To protect our own rights, safety, or stuff.

Also, all outside partners are required to protect your info.

**6. Links to Other Sites**

If you click a link and end up on some other website, we don't have any control over their privacy stuff.

Read their privacy policy before you do anything.

**7. Email Stuff**

If you sign up for our newsletter or get in touch, we might send you email.

You can stop the emails anytime by clicking unsubscribe on the email.

Also, we don't send spam or give your email to marketing companies.

**8. Kids' Privacy**

Our site is for everyone, but not really aimed at kids under 13.

We don't knowingly gather info from kids. If we find out we did, we'll delete it asap.

**9. How Long We Keep Your Info**

We only keep your personal data is needed unless you ask us to delete it.

When the data is no longer usefull, it will be deleted.

**10. Your Data Rights**

Depending on where you live, you might be able to:

*   See what info we have about you.
*   Ask us to fix or delete your info.
*   Say no to us using your info.
*   Get a copy of your info.

**11. International**

If you're visiting from somewhere else, your info might get sent to a different country to be processed.

We will ensure your data is protection as standard.

**12. Changes to This Policy**

We might change this privacy policy now and then because of laws, tech changes, or just because we're doing things differently.

Check the last updated date at the top and come back every now and then to stay in the loop. If you keep using the site, that means you're okay with the new rules.
